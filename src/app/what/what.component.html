What I’m really thinking about being a public university student

Being a public university student is kind of challenging. First, you have to present an exam to be admitted, and sometimes it makes you go blank with several hard questions. And the real matter it’s not just to approve it, but obtaining a certain mark so that you can enter to your desired degree. Therefore, if you want to be part of the university, you need to demonstrate that you're capable of doing it, and, even more, being able to get good marks and maintain your place there.
On the other hand, even if you've made your best effort to enter university, you’ll have to face all the institutional issues such as economic deficit, infrastructure problems and student strikes along your stancy here. Furthermore, we as public university students are thought to be troublesome and rebels, and that’s why people, who have never been in contact with public education, look down on us and tend to be rude when we express our opinion about these topics.
Nonetheless, professionals of this university are the ones who oftenly get the best jobs when they apply for big companies. And that’s due to the fact that the academic level is very high and most of the subjects in pensums are really demanding, as well as professors are highly qualified and always try to give their best for students, motivating them to be successful in their own career ladders.
