Humans of UNAL In 2019 I was determinedto do a big change in my life, that was one of my big purposes on new year’s eve. Since the middle of the year, my mom started proposing the idea of having an adopted dog and giving it a family. She has always got a heart of gold.
In August my mom received a call from one of her best friends, a woman named Martha who I didn't like because of her annoying personality and her bad treatment of people. Martha told my mom that she had adopted a dog from the street but she couldn't have it anymore, because she didn't have more space in her house. Its name was Forky because of her children, who had recently seen Toy story 4 and decided to name the dog as one of the movie’s characters.
Forky moved to my house two days after the call (on August 19th), and that day is still the best moment that I remember so far. At the beginning, Forky was such an introverted dog. He was scared all the time, maybe because of his past life on the streets, but as the time passed, it entered in confidence with us, and now it is the most loyaland affectionatedog. I have learned from Forky’s story that beautiful experiences and moments sometimes come from who you do not expect.
